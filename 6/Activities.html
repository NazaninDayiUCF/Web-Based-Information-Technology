Activities at Atlantic Trails

Hiking

Atlantic Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Tour Start Time Description Guide Time
6:00 am 2 miles hike on the upper ridge-watch the sun rise. Stepanie 2 hours
8:00 pm 4 mile hike in the state park.Transportation leaves from main office Roger 3 hours
1:00 pm 6 mile hike over the full trail and the beach head.Some light climbing Stepanie or Heidi 4.5 hours

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

Kayak Type Description Skill Level
Sea Long,flat bottom,cargo area for your personal effects. Low to moderate
Surf Shorter, more maneverable than the sea kayak. Moderate to high
White Water Very short, rounded hull, very robust.Highly maneuverable. Expert

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Atlantic trails, we offer guided bird watching tips at sunrise several times a week

Tour Start Time Description Commonly Seen Birds Guide
5:30 am Tuesday Only-September thru November 1.5 mile hike on the upper ridge. Lasts about 1 hour Green Herons,Nothern Harriers,Ospreys Stephanie
6:00 am Saturday and Sunday Only-March thru June 3 mile hike on the beach.Lats about 2 hours Cormorants,Canadian Geese,Least Sandpiper Heidi
5:30 am Thursday Only- June thru Agust 3 mile hike in the State Park. Some light climbing Lesser Yellowlegs,Willet, Black-bellied Plover Stephanie or Heidi
