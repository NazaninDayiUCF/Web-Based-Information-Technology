Enjoy Nature in Luxuary

What is a bungalow?

Our luxuary bungalows are permanent structures four feet off the ground.Each bungalow has canvas walls, a wooden floor. and a roof dome that can be opened.

how are the bungalows furnished?

Each bungalow is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxuary camping experince also includes electicity and a sink with hot and cold running water.Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most quests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
